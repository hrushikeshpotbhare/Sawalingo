Activities 

Sawali Sevabhavi Sanstha is at present working in the following fields 

  • Health Centers with HIV/AIDS and Cancer Detection camps. (SMP centers)
  • Help to Mentally challenged children and physically challenged person.
  • Help to Mentally challenged children and physically challenged person.
  • Increase and improve the access to safe drinking water and sanitation.
  • Conservation of Environment with Watershed Development.
  • Supporting Organization in Jalswarajya and Apale Pani Project.
  • Technical supporting Organisation in Bhujal (NRDWP-GSDA) Project.
  • Self-Help Groups (SHGs) with Micro Finance.

  • Basic orientation & skill up-gradation training programs.
  • South development through vocational skill development training programs.

                    Some of our activities in detail :

Jalswarajya Project/Apale Pani Project :
In the rural area of Maharashtra water scarcity is the major problem. Especially between the months of January and June every year. Due to this, women were forced to travel long distances to fetch drinking water. rural people are facing many problems and to salve the major problems of scarcity of water, the govt of India with the help of German govt.started Jalswarajya and Apale Pani project in Maharashtra. 

This project is implemented by Govt. of Maharashtra and supported by German Govt and World Bank. In this project we worked in rural area of nanded, Pune, and Aurangabad Districts We involved In Govt of Maharashtra’s Bhujal ((NRDWP.GSDA) projects for solving the problem of scarcity of drinking water in satara districts.

Agriculture Related Activities :
Watershed Development
By 2011 Sawali ngo started also working in the village. With active involvement of villagers, watershed development works were started. We also working in Soil and water conservation works, In addition to this, Drumstick Plantation, Raising of Nurseries, a forestation improved practises of farmers etc. As a result of this, remarkable results were seen. 

Our Community Development Works like Krushi melavas and trainings. vocational training in milk production, improved practises of farmers etc, Distribution of Improved Seed and Fertilizer, Vegetable Cultivation, Vermi Compost Units, were carried out. To increase and stabilize agricultural income through sustainable development of water and land resources..

We built Vanrai Bandhara, cement Bandharas and CCT in rural area which are very useful in pushing the water level up and filling the wells situated near them. We also constructed 3 Vanrai Bund for water conservation. The project was a huge success. Now villagers get drinking water all year round because the wells never dry up and they produces more production from the agriculture.
